Data Collection and Management
==============================

This section covers the comprehensive data collection methodology, dataset structure, and data management practices used in the I-Coach system.

Overview
--------

I-Coach relies on high-quality exercise video data for training robust machine learning models. Our data collection process ensures diverse representation across different exercise types, form variations, and demographic groups.

Key Components
--------------

- **Video Data Collection**: Systematic recording of exercise movements
- **Pose Estimation**: MediaPipe-based keypoint extraction
- **Data Annotation**: Manual and automated labeling processes
- **Quality Control**: Validation and filtering protocols
- **Dataset Management**: Organization and version control
